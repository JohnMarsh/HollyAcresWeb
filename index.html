About the Band

Hailing from Ottawa, Canada, Holly Acres brings back the raw energy and emotional depth of early 2000s pop punk. With driving guitar riffs, infectious melodies, and lyrics that hit you right in the feels, we're channeling the spirit of the genre's golden era while carving out our own path. Whether you grew up on Warped Tour compilations or you're just discovering what made that era special, Holly Acres delivers the nostalgic punch and heartfelt honesty that defined a generation.
